About Deimon Studios

Deimon Studios is currently an one man army game development company that is entertainment-oriented. The game COR:PS has been developped with Unreal Engine 4 for a bachelor's thesis at the École de technologie Supérieure in Montreal. The first version of the game was made from scratch in about 250 hours total (programming/design). This includes the time spent to learn C++ and UE4.

Download COR:PS

COR:PS is free and will always be. The main goal behind the game is to share the passion I put into creating it.
