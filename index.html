About COR:PS

In Cybergenetic Orion Ranger : Planet Shamang, the player incarnate a cyborg who has the mission to save his planet from invaders. Many invaders intend to destroy the planet Shamang in order to loot its remaining resources.

The player needs to parkour his way around the planet and absorb all the incoming energy beams thrown by the invaders. The Cybergenetic Orion Ranger has a lot of programs integrated in his system in order to travel at high speed.

First, many powerups are scattered on the planet that the cyborg can pick up in order to upgrade his system’s attributes (speed, jump, stamina, absorb). Also, many secrets concerning the cyborg’s system are hidden throughout the planet.

Every ranger must learn all of them in order to compete against the invaders at his full potential.
